1. The Viſor pluckt off FROM Richard Thompſon of Briſtol, Clerk, In a Plain and True Character of Him.

    _ Sir John Lloyd's Certificate concerning Mr. Thompſon, under the Seal of the Office of his Majoralty.

    _ The Lord Biſhop of Chicheſter, lately of Briſtol, his Certificate.

    _ The Dean and Chapter of Sarum, their Certificate.

    _ A Certificate Signed by ſeveral of his Auditors upon the Thirtieth of January, 1679. being Perſons of great Reputation for Loyalty as well as Fortune.
